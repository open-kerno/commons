# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a welcoming and respectful place for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- Assume good intent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Discriminatory jokes or language.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any behavior that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainers at **en.luismiguelbaez@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion. Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions and to ban contributors who do not follow this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community forum associated with this project.
